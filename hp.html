About ADARSHA

--Brief Introduction--

  • 1. A software that has the ability to read and conduct searches of ancient documents in a digital format. There are three main categories of texts: (a) Kangyur (Buddha’s Words); (b) Tengyur (commentaries on Buddha’s Words); and (c) Tibetan Buddhist scriptures.
  • 2. The software features a fast search engine and simple user interface that meets the needs and habits of the common user in searching and reading material. There is the provision of Wylie input and summaries of scriptures for the convenience of use by the academic community.
  • 3. His Holiness the 17th Karmapa Orgyen Trinley Dorje named the software ADARSHA (Sanskrit) which has the meaning of a “clear mirror” with the hope that readers are able to see their own minds as a reflection through studying the scriptures and clearly be able to see the nature of mind just like looking at the clear reflection of a mirror.


--Features--

  • 1. Search: features a table of contents for selection and also gradual search by entering in the name of the scripture,table of contents or text as search entries.
  • 2. View texts: display that allows simple view of text with the ability to adjust text size and spacing etc.
